My Early Years

My name is Kyle O’Bryan I was born on January 2nd, 2002. And have since been living in my hometown of Alexandria Kentucky. I have 2 older Brothers and 2 dogs and a cat. I attended St. Joesph as my grade school, and there I played basketball, soccer, and was even involved in the robotics team for a little bit. I spent my 1st grade through 8th grade there and eventually graduated. After St Joesph, I decided to continue my studies at Bishop Brossart High School. At Bishop Brossart, I played basketball and soccer and was elected team captain of the soccer team my senior year. Throughout my high school career, I have had many interests and some of those inlude reading, playing sports, and learning about technology.

My Middle Years

After Highschool and some careful planning and thinking, I decided to attend Northern Kentucky University where I am still currently enrolled. I have always had a huge interest in computers and in fact, I have built five PCs from scratch. And because of my love for computers, I knew I wanted to major in something like that. So I decided on Cyber Security because I believe it is such an important job, especially in today’s world.

My current Life

I’m a Bengals and Noterdam fan, I enjoy going to college football, and basketball games with my friends as well as NFL games with my dad and brothers.
